The Best Soup Ever

Rating Img Here?

Author: Eric

Prep Time: Not Long

Cook Time: Now

Total Time: Just Make The Soup

Yield: 1 Serving. Too good not to eat it all.

Method: With a Kitchen

Cuisine: Good

Ingredients

  • 1 quart of stock
  • 3 onions
  • 2 cans of tomatoes
  • 5 medium sized potatoes
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 4 oz of milk
  • 16 oz of pasta
  • 2 tsp of basil
  • 1 tablespoon of apple vinegar
  • 2 tablespoon of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Place the following ingredients into pot: Stock, Onions, Tomatoes, Olive Oil, Salt, Pasta, Basil, and Potatoes
  2. Cover with lid and cook on high pressure for 5 minutes. Make certain to add the liquid before you begin to heat it.
  3. Slowly stir in Milk and Garlic a little at a time to make a great stew
  4. Let cool for another 5 minutes if you can bear it. Enjoy!
